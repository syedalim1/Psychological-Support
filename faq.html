What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive suite of mental health support services including:

  • Personalized mental health assessments and checkups
  • Evidence-based self-help resources (articles, tools, exercises)
  • Tailored recommendations for improving wellbeing
  • Multiple support channels (chat, email, phone, video)
  • Digital wellness journal with mood tracking
  • Moderated community forums
  • Guided meditation and mindfulness sessions
  • Goal setting and habit formation tools
Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality and security are our top priorities. All communications and personal data are handled with strict privacy measures in accordance with HIPAA regulations.

Our platform uses end-to-end encryption for all communications, and your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard protections. Our licensed professionals adhere to strict ethical guidelines regarding client confidentiality.

You can review our detailed privacy policy here.

How do I get started?

Getting started with our platform is simple:

  1. Click the "Get Started" button on the homepage or use the "Register" link
  2. Create your secure account with email verification
  3. Complete a brief initial assessment to help us understand your needs
  4. Explore your personalized dashboard to access assessments, resources, and support options
  5. Schedule a welcome call with one of our team members (optional but recommended)

The entire setup process takes less than 10 minutes, and you'll have immediate access to our resources.

How quickly can I speak with a professional?

Our response times vary by support channel:

  • Chat support: Available 24/7 with typical response times under 5 minutes
  • Email support: Responses within 24 hours, often much sooner
  • Scheduled video/phone sessions: Usually available within 48 hours
  • Urgent support: Immediate connection to crisis resources

For emergency situations, please call your local emergency services or crisis hotline immediately.

What are your subscription plans and costs?

We offer several flexible subscription options to meet different needs:

  • Basic Plan ($29/month): Access to self-help resources, assessments, and limited chat support
  • Standard Plan ($59/month): Everything in Basic plus unlimited chat support and two video sessions per month
  • Premium Plan ($99/month): Everything in Standard plus priority scheduling, additional video sessions, and personalized progress reports

We also offer annual subscriptions at a 20% discount and have special rates for students, seniors, and healthcare workers.

Many insurance plans cover our services. You can verify your coverage here.

What qualifications do your professionals have?

All our mental health professionals are licensed and credentialed with extensive experience in their fields. Our team includes:

  • Licensed Clinical Psychologists (PhD/PsyD)
  • Licensed Professional Counselors (LPC)
  • Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSW)
  • Licensed Marriage and Family Therapists (LMFT)

Each professional undergoes rigorous background checks, credential verification, and continuous education. Many specialize in specific areas such as anxiety, depression, relationship issues, trauma, and more.

You can view detailed profiles of our team members in the "Meet Our Team" section.

Can I remain anonymous when using your services?

We understand that privacy is important. While complete anonymity has limitations due to security and quality of care, we offer options:

  • You can use a pseudonym for most platform features
  • Our community forums allow anonymous participation
  • You can browse resources without creating a profile

For personalized support, we require basic identifying information to provide appropriate care and ensure security, but this information is strictly protected under our privacy policy.

What devices can I use to access your platform?

Our platform is accessible across multiple devices:

  • Web browser: Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge
  • Mobile app: Available for iOS and Android devices
  • Tablet: Optimized interface for iPad and Android tablets

All your data syncs seamlessly across your devices, so you can start a session on your phone and continue on your computer later. Our platform is also fully accessible for users with disabilities and complies with WCAG 2.1 guidelines.

What if I need help outside of scheduled sessions?

We offer multiple support options between scheduled sessions:

  • 24/7 chat support with qualified professionals
  • Self-help resources available anytime
  • Community support forums moderated by professionals
  • Automated mood tracking and coping suggestions

For urgent situations, we provide direct connections to crisis resources including the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (988) and local emergency services.

Remember, if you're experiencing a medical or psychological emergency, please call emergency services (911) or go to your nearest emergency room immediately.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. When you cancel:

  • You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period
  • No additional charges will be made after cancellation
  • Your data remains secure and available if you choose to return

Cancellation can be done directly from your account settings or by contacting our support team. We also offer subscription pauses for temporary breaks.
